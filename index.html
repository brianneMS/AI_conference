Overview & Learning Outcomes

Academic advising is a shared responsibility. Use this syllabus each quarter to understand advisor-student expectations, plan your course of study, and access resources.

  • Learn to access advising staff, resources, and tools.
  • Create and use an Educational Plan to move toward completion.
  • Clarify and prepare for educational and career goals.
  • Understand academic policies & important timelines.
Primary Advisors & Areas of Study ▸

Advisors are assigned by Area of Study. Common areas: Arts, Health, Culinary, Education, Social Sciences, Business, Skilled Trades, BTS, STEM.

Responsibilities — Student & Advisor ▸

Student expectations: Prepare for appointments, check email, follow through on recommendations, meet before registration opens.

Advisor commitments: Provide a safe respectful space, guide decisions, recommend classes, share resources, follow FERPA.

Policies & Procedures ▸

Key policies include Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP), Academic Student Progress, Student Complaints procedure, and the Academic Calendar. See the "Links" panel for direct resources.
